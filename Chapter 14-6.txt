Cavs ring in New Year with 122-110 win over LeBron James and Los Angeles Lakers - cleveland.com

The Cavs' win over the Lakers in the first round of the playoffs, which came with a home-court advantage, was the only result of a late-game game under LeBron James.

James, who was out with a bruised left foot, was called for a technical foul after trailing by as many as three points.

James went on to score 22 points and take 15 assists in the second half.

The Lakers scored 40 points and led by as many as 11 in the second half.

The Lakers were able to break the lead in the third quarter and open up a 12-point lead with 3:38 to play.

James scored 19 points and the Lakers finished with 24.
